Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

By using Pleader AI, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care.

1. Who We Are

Pleader AI ("we", "us", or "our") is an AI-powered legal intelligence platform designed for the Pakistani legal community. Our platform is accessible at www.pleaderai.com. For any privacy-related questions, you may contact us at privacy@pleaderai.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you register or sign in via Google.
  • Usage Data: Documents you generate, legal queries you submit, tools you use, and session activity within the platform.
  • Uploaded Files: Documents (PDFs, images) you upload for extraction or analysis. These are processed transiently and are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Payment Information: When you upgrade a plan, we collect transaction reference numbers and payment method (JazzCash / EasyPaisa). We do not store full bank account or card numbers.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and cookies used to operate and improve the service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Pleader AI platform and its features.
  • Process your legal document generation requests through our AI systems.
  • Manage your account, subscription, and usage limits.
  • Send service-related notifications (e.g., plan upgrades, usage alerts).
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with applicable Pakistani laws and regulations.

4. Document Confidentiality

We understand that legal documents are highly sensitive. Documents and queries you submit are sent to our AI processing service (Anthropic Claude) solely to generate your requested output. We do not use the content of your legal documents to train AI models, and we do not share your documents with any third party except as required to deliver the service.

Your uploaded documents are processed in memory and are not stored permanently. Generated documents exist only in your session unless you explicitly save them.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party services that help us operate Pleader AI (e.g., Supabase for authentication and database, Anthropic for AI processing, Vercel for hosting). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by Pakistani law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Usage logs are retained for up to 12 months for operational and security purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@pleaderai.com.

7. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in and to remember your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the platform.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, secure authentication (via Supabase), and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and keep your account credentials private.

9. Children's Privacy

Pleader AI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@pleaderai.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on the platform or sending an email to your registered address. Continued use of Pleader AI after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
