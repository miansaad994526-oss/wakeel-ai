Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

We want you to be satisfied with Pleader AI. If you are not happy with your subscription, please read this policy to understand your options.

1. Free Plan

The Free plan is available at no cost and does not involve any financial transaction. No refunds are applicable for the Free plan.

2. Paid Subscriptions — Refund Window

We offer a 7-day refund window for first-time paid subscribers (Professional or Firm plan). If you are not satisfied with your plan within 7 days of your first payment, you may request a full refund by contacting us at billing@pleaderai.com.

✓ Eligible for Refund

First-time subscribers within 7 days of initial payment, where the service was materially non-functional or failed to deliver advertised features.

✗ Not Eligible for Refund

Renewals after the first billing cycle, partial-month usage after 7 days, or where the account was suspended due to Terms violations.

3. Renewal Charges

Monthly and annual subscriptions renew automatically. We send a reminder email 3 days before renewal. If you wish to cancel before renewal, you must do so prior to the renewal date. Charges already processed for a new billing cycle are generally non-refundable except at our discretion.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us with the following information:

  • Your registered email address.
  • The payment method used (JazzCash / EasyPaisa).
  • Your transaction reference number or screenshot.
  • Reason for the refund request.

Refund requests are processed within 5–7 working days. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method.

5. Service Disruptions

If Pleader AI experiences an extended outage (more than 48 consecutive hours) due to our fault, affected subscribers may be eligible for a proportional credit or partial refund. Please contact us if you believe you are affected.

6. Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. Unauthorised chargebacks may result in account suspension while the dispute is resolved.

7. Contact for Billing

Billing & Refund Support

Email: billing@pleaderai.com
Response time: within 2 business days.
